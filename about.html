About Safe Paws

Safe Paws is an initiative dedicated to managing stray dogs with compassion and innovation. We aim to bridge the gap between technology and humane animal care — making cities safer while ensuring animal welfare through smart reporting and coordination.

Our goal is to create a balanced environment where humans and animals can coexist peacefully. Join us in making a difference by being part of a compassionate community.
